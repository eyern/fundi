About Fundi

Fundi bridges the gap between aesthetic vision and reliable property maintenance. We serve clients in Nairobi and surrounding areas. We provide end-to-end interior transformations, eliminating the stress of coordinating between separate designers and contractors.

Our in-house team includes licensed professionals, creative designers, and certified handymen who turn design concepts into beautiful, functional realities.
